# Sponsorship Category Policy — v1

Resolved 2026-07-21 (plan open decision #7). Industry-standard (IAB-aligned) exclusion
list adapted to Klimr: an 18+ verified-identity sports community whose core asset is
**ranking integrity**. Enforceable mirror: `lib/sponsorship-categories.ts` — the future
sponsorship engine validates against it. Money handling is **recorded-only** in v1 (no
payments through Klimr), so this policy governs *who may appear as a sponsor at all*.

## Prohibited — never accepted, no review path

| Category | Why |
| --- | --- |
| Gambling & sports betting | Betting on amateur matches is a direct threat to the match-integrity and ranking systems. Prohibited outright, unlike pro-league norms. |
| Adult & sexual content | Incompatible with a community sports environment. |
| Tobacco, nicotine & vaping | Universally excluded from sport sponsorship. |
| Illegal & recreational drugs | No compliant placement exists. |
| Weapons, firearms & ammunition | Standard community-platform exclusion. |
| Payday & predatory lending | Exploitative; reputational risk. |
| MLMs & get-rich-quick schemes | Deceptive by structure. |
| Political campaigns & advocacy | Klimr venues stay neutral ground. |
| Prescription pharmaceuticals | Regulated DTC category out of scope. |
| Supplements with medical claims (incl. SARMs/peptides) | Unsubstantiated claims; doping-adjacent. |
| Hate or extremist organizations | Zero tolerance. |
| Counterfeit goods | Illegal; harms legitimate sponsors. |
| Token offerings & speculative investments | High-fraud category. |

## Restricted — manual review with conditions

| Category | Conditions |
| --- | --- |
| Alcohol | 18+ platform permits; must respect venue rules; never adjacent to coaching-of-minors contexts. |
| CBD & hemp wellness | Per-jurisdiction legal review; no medical claims. |
| Licensed financial services | Review confirms licensing. |
| Energy drinks & claim-free supplements | Review screens for medical claims. |
| Weight-loss programs | Review screens for body-shaming or extreme methods. |

## Review model

Restricted categories require admin review at sponsor onboarding (Tier-2 business
verification is the natural gate). The category taxonomy is versioned here; changes
append a new version section rather than editing v1.
